Paris

Last but certainly not least: Paris. The list could go on forever, but here are a few of our favorite spots — places we genuinely love and return to.

11ème arrondissement

  • Au Passage - Modern small plates in a lively setting
  • Café de l'Industrie - Relaxed bistro serving classic French dishes
  • Les Oeillets - Natural wine bar

6ème arrondissement

  • Le Petit Lutetia - Elevated French classics in a traditional setting

1er arrondissement

  • Rue Saint Anne - The go-to street for Japanese and Korean food. Try Tonton Ramen for excellent ramen.
