Driving

We recommend driving as that will be the fastest way to travel if you are coming from Paris.

Public transport

If you wish to get public transport from Paris, we recommend taking the ligne J from Gare Saint-Lazare to Épône Mézières (45 min journey). You will the need to get a 20 min taxi to the venue.

You can also get other train lines to Mantes-la-Jolie, which is also located a 20 min car drive from the venue.
